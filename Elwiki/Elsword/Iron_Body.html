[Active] For a certain duration, gain Super Armor status. Received damage is reduced, and movement speed is increased greatly.
Iron Body
  • Super Armor status
  • Received Damage Reduction
  • Movement Speed Increased by 1.3 times
Pain Endurance
  • All debuffs are removed upon activation.
Final Enhanced Skill
  • Damage reduction effect is increased and a [Pain Endurance] skill is additionally granted.

Tips and Details

  • All debuffs (Stun, Flattened, Bound, Poison, etc) can still activate after the activation of Iron Body.
  • There are a couple of frames left between the activation and the effect of the skill where you can still hit Elsword, canceling the skill and forcing it into cooldown (though the red super armor outline may persist due to lag or some other reason).
  • You are still affected by KD calculations, so if you hit the KD limit, you will still fall down.
  • Received Damage Decrease is multiplicative.


Trivia

  • This skill works identically to Elesis' Iron Body - Strong, but is more focused on defensive effects.
  • When using in Free Training, Iron Body doesn't show in Skill Damage tab.
