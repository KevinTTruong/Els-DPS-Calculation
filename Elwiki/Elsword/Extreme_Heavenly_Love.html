[Couple] Recover HP and MP using the Power of Love.


Requirements

Characters Master Level Required Skill Requirement
Any Base Job Class Lv. 2 1 (Just)Couple


Skill Information

Skill Level Requirement HP Recovery MP Recovery  Couple BuffExtreme Heavenly Love Range MP Usage Cooldown
MP Recovery per Second Duration
1 (Just)Couple 50% 50% 10% 5 Seconds 1500 0 MP 300 Seconds
2 (M) Married Couple 100% 100% 10 Seconds


Tips and Details

  • This skill can be used in Sparring rooms but not in official PvP.
  • Please be aware that when throwing the heart, it will be tossed at a small distance in front of you. If you were to walk away from it before it hits the ground, you will be out of its range and will not receive the recovery effects.
  • Extreme Heavenly Love can be used in Henir's Time and Space Challenge mode.
  • The HP/MP Recovery affects only you and your partner. Party members will not be affected.
  • This skill can be used when you're not with your partner.
  • This skill can be canceled if you're attacked before the heart is thrown.
