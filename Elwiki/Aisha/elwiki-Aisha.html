Background

Main Article: Ruben Forest (Aisha)
  • In-game Version
  • Nexon Version

A powerful mage in her own right, Aisha became a Great Magician from a young age.

While traveling with her grandfather, a great sage, she find an ancient ring from a magical ancient site that absorbs most of her powers.

With most of her power gone, Aisha sets off on a journey to recover her powers by tracing the magical signature of the missing ring.

Faster MP Regeneration

Aisha's MP Regeneration

Aisha can regenerate 10 MP per second by standing still. Her MP Regeneration is much faster than Rena's, who regenerates 5 MP per second. She can enter her mana regeneration state after standing still for half a second.

Tips and Details

  • Aisha falls at a much faster speed than most other characters when is held down after jumping or pressed while levitating.
    • Furthermore, if Aisha is attacked during levitation, she will proceed to fall at a much faster speed as well.


Trivia

  • Aisha's job path in Lore/Story would be the Aether Sage path.
  • Aisha's official theme song is In the Violet Hour.
  • Aisha's last name is revealed to be Landar in the side story Lost Power 4.
  • The ring which stole Aisha's power is called the Ring of Mimir, which is named after Mímir, a mythical figure from Norse mythology, said to possess great knowledge and wisdom.
    • In the current story, the ring was stolen from her by the Mysterious Man after it absorbed her powers. This was initially revealed during Lost Power 5 and later incorporated into the Epic Quests in [Village] From Hagus to Hoffman. Despite this, her Prologue was not rewritten, and still states that the ring merely disappeared.
  • Aisha is afraid of creepy things, such as spiders and ghosts.
  • Curiously, Aisha's Promotion avatar has a belt that's larger than her waist, yet it does not fall off nor dangle; it is likely secured by magic.
  • Aisha's Promotional weapon changes with Awakening Mode turning the end of the staff to a heart-like shape.
  • Aisha is the only character (excluding Rose) whose two initial class paths focus on the same stat type, both Aether Sage and Oz Sorcerer being Magical Attack classes.
  • During the development of the game, Aisha was originally named Arme, who was based on a character with the same name in a separate MMO created by KoG called Grand Chase.
    • It can still be found within the game's core files, as the scripts within her .kom files all go by the name Arme instead of Aisha.
    • In the beta, before she was renamed to Aisha, she was voiced by the same voice actor of Arme in Grand Chase.
    • The faster MP regeneration while standing still originated from Arme in Grand Chase.
    • Many of her skills included in Elsword are a reference to Arme's skills in Grand Chase, some of them even share the exact names. However, some of those occurrences may be a result of retiring the character concept.
  • Aisha wrote the Skill Notes "Endless Desire II" for Ara's Energy Absorption skill, "Effective Ways to Ignite Fire" for Elesis' Explosive Fist, and "Slap" for Heaven's Fist - Sweeper.
  • Aisha's canon birthday is the same as the release date for Elsword in Korea. She also shares her birthday with Elsword.
  • A running gag among the game development staff is Aisha being flat-chested, much to her dismay. In Volume 4 of ElType, Aisha was not too pleased to still have a flat chest upon "aging" to an Elemental Master. Also, in the same volume, Aisha expressed envy towards Rena's chest size upon her advancement to Grand Archer, where her breasts become notably larger.
    • However, Aisha in-game model does not display her with a flat chest, some outfits actually add more curves, though this is likely a game model/story discrepancy.
